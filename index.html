About

I am a builder who likes to explore. I take things apart, learn how they work, and make new ones.

Most of what I explore is AI. I build search and retrieval, plug models into real products, and I have trained and run my own models in Python and TensorFlow. I like getting a model to work well inside a real product, not just a demo.

The other half is physical. I build robotics and small hardware, from boards and sensors to home automation, and I take part in robot battles. It is the quickest way to see if an idea really works.

I have worked in software for ten years, much of it leading teams and shaping how systems fit together. But the part I enjoy most is exploring. This site is simply where I publish the things I research and build, because I like to share them with people who are curious too.

About

I build the parts of products other people rely on. The data models, the APIs, the admin panels, the infrastructure, and now the AI underneath them.

I have worked in software for over ten years, most of it as a solution architect and lead developer. I work mainly in Laravel and React on top of PostgreSQL, with Python across automation, data, and machine learning. I like strict schemas, reusable abstractions, and interfaces that are dense with information but still calm to use.

I also run the infrastructure: Docker and Kubernetes, CI/CD, and the observability that catches problems before users do. A growing part of my work is AI, from retrieval pipelines and model integration to training and serving my own models.

Alongside client and product work I build my own things, from internal tools to robotics and IoT, with custom boards and home automation. The experiments below come out of that habit of building things to understand them.
